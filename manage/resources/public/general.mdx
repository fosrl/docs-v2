Use the **General** tab on a public resource to control baseline resource settings.

## General Settings

This section includes:

- **Enable Resource**: Toggle resource visibility on/off.
- **Name**: Display name of the resource.
- **Identifier**: Internal resource identifier.
- **Domain**: Current domain/URL with **Edit Domain** action.

Use **Save Settings** after making changes.

## Maintenance Mode

The General tab also includes a maintenance section to temporarily show a maintenance page to visitors.

- **Enable Maintenance Mode**: Toggle maintenance behavior for the resource.
- Advanced maintenance page behavior and modes are documented on the dedicated maintenance page.

For complete maintenance behavior and limitations, see [Maintenance Page](/manage/resources/public/maintenance).
